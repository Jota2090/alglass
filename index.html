Services

In this section, you can review the services we offer in the commercial and residential areas.

Sliding Shower door

Consist of 2 or more glass panels that slide over a rail allowing them to open and close without scatterring in the bathroom.

Hinge Shower door

Hinge shower doors have the unique feature of having a pivoting mechanism. This is located on a vertical axis on the sides of the doors, so the door can be opened or closed from either the inside or outside where it is located.

Glass Panel

A fixed glass shower panel or shower screen can be a great way to add style and functionality to your bathroom.

Mirror (Residencial)

Choosing the ideal mirror for your bathroom may seem like a simple task, but in reality there are many factors to consider to ensure that it is not only functional, but also a decorative element that complements your space.

Led Mirror (Residencial)

LED mirrors are gaining popularity due to their efficient lighting and modern aesthetics. This type of mirror not only illuminates your face evenly, but also adds a touch of sophistication to your bathroom.

Organic Mirror (Residencial)

Irregularly shaped and inspired by the movements of the water, the organic mirrors are the new fashionable mirrors in the deco. They have the perfect fluidity to add a blow to any space in the house.

Commercial Mirror

Transform the atmosphere of your hotels, commercial properties, beauty salons and barbershops with our premium selection of mirrors.

Commercial Tempered Glass
